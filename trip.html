Welcome to Karnataka!

From majestic palaces and ancient temples to lush forests and vibrant cities, Karnataka offers an unforgettable journey for every traveler.

Top Destinations

Mysore Palace

Experience the grandeur of the royal Mysore Palace, a stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture and the heart of Dasara celebrations.

Hampi

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Hampi, known for its ancient ruins, boulder-strewn landscapes, and rich Vijayanagara history.

Coorg

Relax in the misty hills of Coorg, famous for its coffee plantations, waterfalls, and serene natural beauty.

Gokarna Beaches

Unwind on the pristine beaches of Gokarna, a tranquil alternative to Goa with scenic coastlines and spiritual vibes.

Culture & Heritage

Karnataka boasts a rich tapestry of art, music, dance, and festivals. Witness the vibrant Mysuru Dasara, classical Carnatic music, Yakshagana performances, and intricate handicrafts.

Cuisine

Indulge in Karnataka's diverse cuisine: from spicy Bisi Bele Bath and crispy Mysore Masala Dosa to sweet Mysore Pak and aromatic Coorgi Pandi Curry.
